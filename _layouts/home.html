About the group

Based in Lorena · São Paulo

ComputEEL/MatSci is a Computational Materials Science group at the Materials Engineering Department (DEMAR) of EEL/USP. We work on superconductivity, magnetism and thermodynamics of alloys, focusing on ab initio simulations, CALPHAD modelling and thermal properties of alloys and intermetallics.

  • Coordinator: Prof. Luiz T. F. Eleno
  • Full team plus national and international collaborators
  • Publications in Nature Communications, PRB, CALPHAD and more

Focus areas

Quantum materials

Superconductors, magnetism, phonon spectra and electron–phonon coupling.

Thermodynamics & phases

Phase diagrams, alloy stability, heat capacity and phase transitions.

Digital tools

Reproducible workflows, visualizations and scripts to speed up research.
